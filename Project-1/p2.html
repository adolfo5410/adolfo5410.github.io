Coffee Beans

Robusta

The is cheaper and stronger than arabica due to its bitter flavor. Typically used to for espresso drinks and instant coffee mix. very high caffeine content.

Arabica

This is the most popular style of coffee bean. Typically used for black coffee it is sweeter and has a greater taste. However it is more expensive and has less caffeine content.
