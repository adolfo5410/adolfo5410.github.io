Coffee Beans

Coffee comes in many forms, here are a few very popular drink choices.

Black Coffee

Just simple coffee, ground coffee beans steeped in hot water.

Espresso

Espresso shots are served by itself or used in other drinks, very strong.

Latte

Very popular choice, a latte has a shot of espresso and steamed milk with foam, and can be flavored with anything like vanilla.

Mocha

Mocha is a great choice as it has chocolate. expresso and steamed milk with foam.
